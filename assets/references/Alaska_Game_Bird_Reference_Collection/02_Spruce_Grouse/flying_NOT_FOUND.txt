SPRUCE GROUSE — FLYING IMAGE NOT INCLUDED

A clearly reusable, correctly identified, real photograph of a Spruce Grouse in true flight could not be located after searches of Wikimedia Commons, U.S. government media, and the broader web. Stock photographs were found, but they were not included because their licenses do not permit redistribution in this collection. This note is included instead of substituting a different grouse species, a bird merely perched with wings open, or an unlicensed image.
